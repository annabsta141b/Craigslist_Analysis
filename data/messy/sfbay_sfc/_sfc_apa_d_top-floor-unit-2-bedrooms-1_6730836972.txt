Nob Hill is one of San Francisco's signature neighborhoods, renowned for its city landmarks and the famous hotels that border Huntington Park. For those who explore further, it also reveals unexpected facets. In addition to its rich character, the area is influenced by the diverse personalities of the downtown neighborhoods that surround it, making it an intriguing place to visit. 
Cable Car Museum: The City by the Bay boasts the only operating cable car system in the world; if you want to be a real San Franciscan, it pays to brush up on your cable car facts. Favorite restaurants in the neighborhood include Big 4 Restaurant, Cafe Bean, Fleur de Lys, Swan Oyster Depot, Son's & Daughter's, and Miller's East Coast West Delicatessen. For nightlife lovers, Bigfoot Lodge, Cafe Royale, Hyde Out, and the Tonga Room and Hurricane Car are favorite hotspots in the neighborhood!
